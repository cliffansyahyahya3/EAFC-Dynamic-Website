Player of the month

March
Vinicius Jr.

Real Madrid have announced that Vinicius Jr has won their Player of the Month award for March. The Brazilian winger beat out competition from the likes of Karim Benzema, Fede Valverde, and Marco Asensio for the award. Vinicius was involved in seven games across all competitions for Madrid in February, in which he scored six goals and provided three assists.

CROSS-PLAY UPDATE

In EA SPORTS FC™ 24, cross-play is expanding into all multiplayer online modes. Players will now be able to team up with their cross-platform friends as well as play against mixed cross-platform teams across all modes where crossplay is available.

EA connect will continue to be the main social hub where you will be able to manage game invites and your friends across platforms.

When entering EA SPORTS FC™ 24 for the first time cross-play will be enabled by default, unless otherwise prohibited by your personal settings. To send game invites, this will be the same as last year: You will interact with the full EA connect view in Clubs and Volta Football to invite your friends
